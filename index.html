Technologies Studios works for difficult situations with simple solutions both from a technological and social point of view, to bring direct benefit to people and make a difference even in the most adverse situations.

- 00DXV

Cross-Server/Game AND Cross-Roblox/Discord/Other text based communications platform for near instant communication. Good for communicating between different bases / "deployments" in real time without breaking immersion. As an example, a group could have a radio station at their main base, and have a radio in their operational games. Communication from the operational game to the main base can be made to request various forms of support. Customized Radio Networks using simulated dedicated frequencies, listen in to ANY other frequency in use by the platform. Use Cryptographic keys to secure your networks from intrusion!
